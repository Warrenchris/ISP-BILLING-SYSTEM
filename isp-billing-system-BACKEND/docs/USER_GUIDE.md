# ISP Billing System - User Guide

This guide provides instructions for customers on how to use the ISP Billing System.

## 1. Account Management

### 1.1 Registration

To register for a new account, navigate to the registration page and provide the required information:

*   **First Name**
*   **Last Name**
*   **Email Address**
*   **Phone Number** (e.g., +2547XXXXXXXX or 07XXXXXXXX)
*   **National ID**
*   **County**
*   **City**
*   **Password** (must be at least 8 characters long, contain at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one special character)

After successful registration, you will receive a confirmation email.

### 1.2 Login

To log in, enter your registered email address and password on the login page.

### 1.3 Profile Management

Once logged in, you can view and update your profile information, including your personal details and password.

## 2. Data Plans and Subscriptions

### 2.1 Viewing Data Plans

You can browse available data plans, including details such as data limit, price, validity, and features.

### 2.2 Subscribing to a Data Plan

To subscribe to a data plan, select your desired plan and proceed to payment.

### 2.3 Managing Subscriptions

View your active and past subscriptions. You can also cancel your subscription if needed.

## 3. Payments

### 3.1 M-Pesa Payments

The system supports M-Pesa STK Push for convenient payments. When making a payment, you will receive a prompt on your M-Pesa enabled phone to complete the transaction.

### 3.2 Payment History

View a detailed history of all your payments, including transaction IDs, amounts, and status.

## 4. Invoices

### 4.1 Viewing Invoices

Access and download your invoices for your subscriptions. Invoices provide a breakdown of charges and payment status.

## 5. Data Usage Tracking

### 5.1 Real-time Usage

Monitor your real-time data usage to keep track of your consumption.

### 5.2 Usage History and Analytics

View your historical data usage and analytics to understand your consumption patterns over time.

## Support

If you encounter any issues or have questions, please contact our support team.
